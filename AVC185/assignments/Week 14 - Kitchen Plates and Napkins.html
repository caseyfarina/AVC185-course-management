Project Objective:

Design kitchen tableware demonstrating advanced surface modeling techniques with complex material variations and ceramic details.

Project:

Model to scale from reference, UV unwrap, and paint two sets of plates: Dinner plate and salad plate. An antique plate set that exhibits painted patterns or filigree, and a modern plate set that exhibits edges wear and ceramic detail. 

  • Instruction
    • Model the plates to scale, use coherent topology, and maximize the texel density on the UV map.
    • Select reference for the antique plates and use SP techniques to match the reference.

Use cloth simulation techniques to create four different crumpled napkins. 

  • Use SP to texture the napkin with a cloth surface texture, repeating color pattern, stains, and stitching
