Project Requirements:

  • 1st person Unreal Engine Game Requirements
    • Create at least 2 materials using substance sampler to use in your game.
      • Between the two materials use at least five different substance sample filters 
      • Consider something for floors, consider something for walls
    • Create UI for displaying the collectible count
    • Create a working door and use it at least four times in your level design
    • Model an additional  (one more) (if you don't have an idea, perhaps a rock) object for your game using the High Poly Sculpt, Low Poly bake workflow 
    • Reconstruct your level according to your map from last week. What do you need to add? 
      • Will you create models in Blender or Model Mode in Unreal engine?
