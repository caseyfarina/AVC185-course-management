physics animation examples.unitypackage 

Last week I walked through creating the mountain rode scene in Unity. This week you will design your own 2D animated scene in Unity. You can design the 2D assets in any program that will export a PNG file with an alpha channel. This scene must include keyframe animation and physics based animation.

You can create your sprites in any manner. You can use collage techniques, but do not use stock vector assets.

  • Any program that will export a png with alpha channel
  • Draw on paper
  • take pictures 
  • paint

Any real life assets ( photographs or scanning drawing, paintings, etc with your phone) could be photographed and cut out in Photoshop. A great resource.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hfd0VDL3_Xs

You can use any subject for the scene. A factory works well for this project, but it can be anything you like, try to make it something that represents you as an artist.

Your scene must meet the following requirements.

  • 4 different objects are animated with keyframes
    • DO NOT just move objects with keyframes at a constant speed. Use the keyframes to add bounce, gravity, easing, etc.
  • 3 different objects are animated with  movement scripts
    • Use either linear movement or linear rotation on the Important Unity Scripts/Shaders Resource
  • 4 different objects that animate with physics
  • 2 different spawners dynamically creating objects in your scene
  • 2 different objects that have multiple parts (like the car in the mountain road scene) at least 2 parts should animate
