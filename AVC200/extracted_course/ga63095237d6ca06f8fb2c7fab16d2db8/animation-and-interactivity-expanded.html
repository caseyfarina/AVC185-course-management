Requirements:

You do not have to copy my scene. You need to create your own Sprites as PNGs. If you're feeling less comfortable, stick closer to the example.

An interactive 2D scene that contains the following:

  • An interactive animated bouncing ball with squash and stretch
  • One other object with interactive animation
  • One prefab with continuous animation at different speeds
  • One interactive Spawning object with 2D physics
  • Two pngs that are used to decorate and build the scene.
  • Two of the sprites must be sprite sheets. 

Scripts:

  • _mouseInteraction_OnCollider.cs 
  • _spawnSingleObject.cs
  • AnimationSpeed.cs
  • highlightSprite_OnMouseEnter.cs 

 

Deliverable:

An mp4 of your scene from Unity recorder that demonstrates all of the interactivity and animation in your project.
