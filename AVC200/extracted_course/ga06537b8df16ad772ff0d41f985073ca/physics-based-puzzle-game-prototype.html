Course Objectives

Project: Prototype a physics-based puzzle game that allows players to push objects into goal areas

Requirements

    • Four Distinct Goal areas for four distinct objects
    • One material that uses shadergraph and grunge textures

    • A third person capsule controller
    • A win message in the UI
    • Background music
    • A sound effect when an object reaches the goal area
    • A skybox material
