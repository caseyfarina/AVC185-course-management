  • Substance Sampler Feedback,  Using  Sound  and   Video  in Unreal Engine
    • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2cUp7oTQTnU

Project Objective: Students add collectable objects to the game, create VFX, sound FX, and select background music.

  • Course Competencies
    • Navigate Program Interface and tools.
    • Use principles of animation
    • Create non-linear experiences
    • Create movie clips, navigate timelines
    • Optimize performance
    • Distribute various output formats

Project Requirements:

  • Model and Paint a collectable item for your game.
    • How does this object fit the theme of the game?
    • How did you alter the design of the collectable object to draw the player's attention? Consider using the following
      • Colors
      • Animation
      • Particles
      • lights
      • sound
  • Place at least 10 collectables throughout the level.
    • How does your collectable placement change the player behavior?
  • Add background music to the game
  • Add sounds to the collectable object
  • Add User Interface to the game that lets the player know how many collectables are remaining. 
  • Add at least one video panel to the game.
    • The video must be designed by you using AE, C4D, or Unreal (screen recording?)

Deliverable:

  • Canvas
    • A zip file containing the following
      • An OBS recording of a tour of your Unreal level. Address the following questions during your discussion.
        • Limit to < five  minutes
        • Discuss at least three changes you made to the materials in the game from last week's feedback.
        • Discuss your selection of background music (PLEASE MAKE SURE THE GAME IS LOUDER THAN YOUR VOICE ON THE OBS VIDEO)
          • Turn down the Desktop audio level, please listen to your video BEFORE you upload it.
        • Discuss your collectable design
          • How does it relate to the theme?
          • How did you draw the player's attention to the pickup?
          • How did you model the collectable? Not the program, your artistic ideas
          • How did you paint the collectable? Not the program, your artistic ideas
        • Discuss the video panel you used in the game
          • How did you create the video
          • How does it function in the game?
  • Discord
    • Post your screen recording and comment on at least three other student posts with the following prompt
      • Make a suggestion about another substance that could enhance the visual environment of the game.
