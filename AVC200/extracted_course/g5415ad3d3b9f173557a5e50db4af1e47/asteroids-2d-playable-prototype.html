Requirements:

  • Enemies
    • At least 6 different enemy designs
      • extra challenge: make an enemy that shoots
    • use the intermittentForwardVelocity.cs script to propel enemies in a forward direction
    • use the intermittentTorque.cs script to turn a direction enemy 
  • Hero
    • At least 2 animated parts of the hero
    • Forward thrust particles
      • extra challenge: side thrust particles
    • Use either the health bar, or the health display number. DO NOT put both health scripts on the hero.
  • Game structure
    • Start/Title Message (animated)
    • At least three levels of progressing difficulty
    • Communicate to the player that they are in a new level
      • optional 
        • new backgrounds for each level
        • new music for each level
    • Include a lose message and restart instructions for the player
  • Sound & VFX
    • Projectile firing sound
    • hero damage sound/ vfx
    • enemy explosion sound (find new sounds for different enemies)
    • background music
    • Balance the volume between the sound FX and the music
  • Distribution
    • Build the game for either WebGL or windows and upload to Itch.IO
