Please download Open Broadcast Software https://obsproject.com/download

Each week you'll be required to submit a video presenting the work from that week. I'll ask you to use OBS to create these presentation videos. Each week in class I'll play these videos back and discuss your work. 

How to record a video question using OBS.   https://youtu.be/YkH2wTVZA6U

I strongly encourage everyone to appear on screen (camera on) in the introduction and all of the weekly presentation videos. It is not an official requirement, however, since this is a class that combines "in person" and "online" students appearing on camera allows us all to build a real sense of community. By not turning your camera on you create a sense of asymmetry in the class environment. 

Please address all of the following in your 3 minutes introduction video (mp4 format). 

  • Introduce yourself to the class.
    • How do we say your name?
    • How would you like to be addressed? Pronouns?
  • What are you studying at GCC? What do you hope to get out of this class?
  • What previous experience do you have in Animation or Video Game Design?
  • What do you hope to learn in this class?
  • Spend at least two minutes discussing the art examples you posted on discord as examples of your previous work. Be sure to display the work (or a photograph of the work) in the video.
    • How did you make this work?
    • What was the artistic message?
    • What was challenging?
    • What went well?
