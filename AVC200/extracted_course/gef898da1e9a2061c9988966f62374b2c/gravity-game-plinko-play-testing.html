Procedure:

Use OBS to record yourself playing each game by the other designers in your group.

  • Play for a minimum of 2 minutes for each game
  • Discuss the following while playing
    • Visual Design
      • What is your favorite part of the visual design
      • What is something you would change in the visual design? Could you suggest an addition or new approach?
    • Game Design
      • What is your average score? 
      • Do you feel like the game is too hard or too easy?
      • What would you change in order to make the game more playable?
    • Sound Design
      • Does the music fit the theme?
      • Do the sound effects support the design
      • Do you have any suggestions for the sound design? 
      • Are the sound effects and music in balance?
  • Upload your review video to your school Youtube account and set to "Unlisted"
  • Upload your review as a single video for all of the other designers in your group (this may require some editing).
  • Post the review link to Slack and be sure to @ all of the other members of your group,
