Adding Sound and Particle Effects

Adding effects to the breakout game and the collecting game. The structure is the same in both games, any script with an effect field can be used to instantiate a sound and particle effect. Add at least (more for a better experience) one triggered sound and particle effect to the breakout game and the collecting game.

Add Environmental Particle Effects

Add enhanced environmental particle effect to the collecting game. These are particle effects that are not triggered by the game play but rather serve to enhance the world building experience.  Create at least three distinct effects for the 3d game.
