Project Objective: Students will be introduced to 3D modeling and start to expand the design of the environment in the Puzzle Based Physics game. 

  • Manipulate text and related objects, variables and expressions
  • Create movie clips, navigate timelines
  • Create storyboards, develop concepts, visual style

Requirements:

  • Develop a theme for your game world.
    • Examples: Fruit, Detective, Historical Event
      • Consider ideas that will work WELL with your current modeling skills (simple shapes). \
      • I think the most interesting ideas are the ones that take something simple and twist it "Fruit Detective," for instance.
      • DO NOT USE PRE--EXISTING INTELECTUAL PROPERTY. Please no Mario or Pok√©mon themes. Showcase your ideas!
  • Modeling
    • We are not modeling the main character this week. We are modeling object for the environment. 
    • Create four new objects that the character will push in the game.
    • Create four new static modular world parts
      • For example
      • Wedge, platform, arch, runway etc.
    • Create two new animated objects
      • For example, windmill or swinging objects
    • Create at least one object using the Illustrator ->c4D -> Unity workflow
  • Sound Design
    • Update the Music and win sound to fit your theme
  • Environment Lighting
    • Adjust the lighting to fit your theme. 
      • Lighting Menu
      • Directional light
      • Other lights (not too many)
