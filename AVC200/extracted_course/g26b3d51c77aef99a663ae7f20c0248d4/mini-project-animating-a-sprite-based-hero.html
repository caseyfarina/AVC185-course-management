It's time to add some animation to our Crossy Road hero. You'll need to design your own hero bird. I've provided my sprite sheet as an example. I made it in Illustrator but any program that outputs a png with transparency will work.

You'll need this script: AnimationSwitch.cs  

  

  

First take notes on the animation interface, curves, and dopesheet views in Unity.

https://learn.unity.com/tutorial/working-with-animations-and-animation-curves#600c8a28edbc2a3270428112

I walk through creating animations and organizing them in these two tutorials.

  • IMPORTANT NOTES
    • All the animation happens "In place" do not move the character horizontally or vertically over the course of the entire animation clip.
    • Take care to build the hierarchy well. You can't change this halfway through. You'll need to start over if you need to make a change to the hierarchy

Deliverable:

An mp4 of you playing your game with an animating "bird hero." It doesn't have to be a chicken, but I would stick to biological class Aves. 

The hero should have a forward, up, down, and idle animation. Take some time to balance the speed setting on the player with the speed of the animations.
