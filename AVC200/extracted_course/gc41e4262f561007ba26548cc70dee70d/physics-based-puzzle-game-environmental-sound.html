Project Objective: Students will implement realtime VFX, sound, music, and build a third person 3D game.

  • Course Competencies
    • Navigate Program Interface and tools.
    • Use principles of animation
    • Create non-linear experiences
    • Create movie clips, navigate timelines
    • Optimize performance
    • Distribute various output formats

Project Requirements:

  • Requirements:
    • Add the following to your game
      • >= three environmental sounds in the game
      • one background ambience in a selected part of the game
      • Adjust the volume of the sound FX and the music to balance the audio mix
    • Make revisions based on you classmates feedback

Deliverable:

  • Canvas
    • A zip file containing the following
      • An OBS recording of you playing the built version of your game.
        • Be sure to demonstrate the new environmental sounds
        • Discuss the specific changes you made based on each classmate's feedback
          • List who it was, what they suggested, what changes you made, show them in game
        • Limit to five minutes
  • Discord
    • Post your screen recording and comment on at least three other student posts.
