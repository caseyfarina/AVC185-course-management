Project: 

  1. Create a base mesh for your character design from your game design document.
    • You may need to resketch your character if your sketch was not from the front and side orthographic views.
      • t pose
    • Use "add" menu to add the reference on a plane to your blender scene. 
    • Prioritize the following
      • Simple topology
      • Edge loops around each limb. Alt double click to loop select
      • Symmetrical design
      • Less face/polygon density is more desirable, it is easy to subdivide latter to add more detail
      • Separate mesh objects for the eyes
      • Important modifiers
        • Subdivision Surface
        • Mirror
        • Remesh
        • Smooth
    • Please do not jump ahead and paint the character in Substance Painter this week. We will correct the model and paint the character next week.
