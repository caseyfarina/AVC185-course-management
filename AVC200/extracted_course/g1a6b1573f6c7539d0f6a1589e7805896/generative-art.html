Welcome to your Generative art project! This project asks you to design three different generative scenes in Processing. All three of these scenes should be inspired by a natural location that is particularly inspirational to you. Joshua Tree might be a good example.  Find a picture of this location that speaks to you and use it as the starting point for your generative work. What in nature is generative? Remember this project is not about visually representing that location but rather using that location as the inspiration for generative work. I've included three examples of locations, you must pick your own. You should use the same location for all three sketches.

 

 

 

Requirements

  • Use at least five of your own images
  • Use at least three of your own shapes (SVG)
  • Use at least ONE array
  • Use at least ONE class
  • 1280 x 720
  • All three scenes should be abstract not representational
  • Use a Color Palette
