Project: create a main character for your game.

  • Model your character and consider the proportions and how they will effect the perception of the character
  • Paint the character in Substance Painter. How will the textures align the character in the world you're building?
  • Create the bones (armature) and initial weights for the character in mixamo. https://www.mixamo.com/#/
  • Rig the character in C4D by creating the following controls
    • hips
    • chest
    • hands
    • feet
    • head

Revisions: Make changes to the level objects based on ALL (not just your own) modeling and Substance Painter feedback.

Deliverable:

  • Canvas:
    • A zip containing the following
      • The C4D file of your character
      • The substance painter file of the painted character
      • A pdf of all of your notes to this point in the semester. Be sure to include all of your character modeling and rigging notes
      • A < (less than) three minute OBS recording that details the changes you made to your level design based on feedback.
  • Discord
    • An image of your painted character in Substance Painter
    • Comment on three other student posts. Does the character design fit the theme of the game?
    • Post your revisions video
    • Comment on three other student videos.
