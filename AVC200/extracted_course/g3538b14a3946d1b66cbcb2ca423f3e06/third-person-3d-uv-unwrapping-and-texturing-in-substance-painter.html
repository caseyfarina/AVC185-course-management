Project: UV unwrap the character mesh and texture it in Substance Painter.

  • Make the appropriate changes to your character model based on your feedback and feedback from other students
    • "Save Incremental" so a trail of your previous versions exist incase you need to go back
  • Install the UVTools Blender Addon
  • Create a Checker material for the character using UVtools
  • Enable "Live unwrap"
  • Mark the seems on your character to unwrap
  • "Average Island Scale"
  • "Pack Islands"
  • Export as FBX
  • Import into Substance Painter
  • Use Fill Layers and Paint Layers to Texture the character
