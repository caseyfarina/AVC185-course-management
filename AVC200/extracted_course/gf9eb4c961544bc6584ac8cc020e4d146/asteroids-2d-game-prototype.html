Deliverable:

  1. Two sketches of your theme ideas for your Asteroids game. 
    • Post them on Slack ASAP to receive some feedback. 
    • Each sketch should include a detailed design for the 
      • hero
      • 3 types of enemies
      • environment
    • Consider adding a TWIST to your theme idea for this game. Take a known idea and add a little twist. The following are examples. Please develop your own ideas. 
      1. normal theme idea: Space ------- twist idea: Space Fruit
      2. normal theme idea: Pond------- twist idea: A Pond filled with Bankers
      3. normal them idea: Underwater -------- twist idea: Underwater CyberPunk
  2. Your prototype should include your custom sprites that you've designed for your theme. 
  3. Add the Unity recorder to your project and record your hero flying and shooting enemies. Please upload to Canvas and Slack.
