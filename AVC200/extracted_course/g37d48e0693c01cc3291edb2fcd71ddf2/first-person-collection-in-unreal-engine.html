Project Requirements:

  • Set up a 1st person template in Unreal Engine

    • Create 2 different models (not the collectable) in Blender and use them in your level design
    • Create a collectable in Blender and texture it using Substance Painter
    • Create two animated environment objects and use them multiple times in your level design
