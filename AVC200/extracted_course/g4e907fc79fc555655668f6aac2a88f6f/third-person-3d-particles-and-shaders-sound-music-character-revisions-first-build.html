Project Objective: Students will implement realtime VFX, sound, music, and build a third person 3D game.

  • Course Competencies
    • Navigate Program Interface and tools.
    • Use principles of animation
    • Create non-linear experiences
    • Create movie clips, navigate timelines
    • Optimize performance
    • Distribute various output formats

Project Requirements:

  • Requirements:
    • Add the following to your game
      • Realtime VFX: If you have time explore https://realtimevfx.com/
        • 3 particle effects
          • 2 of the particle effects should be atmospheric
            • fog, fire, magic, sparkles, snow, rain, etc. 
            • Do not put the particle effect in the entire level, rather, use it in a section of your level
          • 1 of the particle effects should be triggered or local
            • you can trigger particles from an event
            • The character could emit particles when 
              • bumped
              • jumping
              • running 
            • A cannon ball could emit a trail
            • anything that moves could have trail of particles
        • One shader based effect
          • water
          • goo
          • lava etc.
      • Sound
        • Background Music
        • Sound Design
          • A sound for when the character jumps
            • use the _keyEventOnDown.cs to send an event when the player jumps. The event could trigger a sound and particles
      • Revise your character animations/modeling/texture painting based on feedback
      • Build your game https://www.youtube.com/live/ifTQtWN7ISI?feature=shared&t=6711 
        • please include the _escapeToQuit.cs in your game
        • Build (not in your project folder)
        • Zip
        • Post on Google Drive
          • Please make accessible to anyone with a link

Deliverable:

  • Canvas
    • A zip file containing the following
      • An OBS recording of you playing the built version of your game. Be sure to show off the new particle effects. If every section of the game is not completely functional right now that is OK. 
        • Limit to three minutes
    • A link to the google drive build of the game
