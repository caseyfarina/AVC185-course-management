Important Terms

  • Modulo - Divide and take the remainder. The "%" is the symbol for modulo. This is great for wrapping movement.
  • || the OR symbol for "if" statements
  • & the AND symbol for "if" statements
  • Use multiplication by "-1" to flip the direction of a speed variable
