Project Objective: Students will create an animated title sequence for a film, show, or game.

  • Course Competencies
    • Compare and contrast the visualization process in traditional arts vs. directing a shot in a
      digital environment. (I)
    • Manipulate aperture, depth of field, lenses, and attributes needed to control a virtual
      camera in various environments. (I-II)
    • Create conceptual sketches, plans and storyboards for a project. (III)
    • Apply lighting and compositional concepts to create a mood in a digital environment. (IV)
    • Determine appropriate camera attributes and shot flow using plans and storyboards. (IV-V)
    • Apply shot flow and directing techniques to a project. (V)
    • Stage a scene for a character and/or product shot. (VI)

Project Requirements:

    • MTVG Title Sequence
      • At least 10 separate shots 
      • Each should appear on screen long enough to read out loud
      • Present the title of the MTVG at either the beginning or end of the sequence
      • Edit the music to begin and end appropriately
      • Edit the sequence to the music
      • Use post processing in Blender and AE to enhance the raw render from Blender
      • Adhere to all of the presented guidelines for typography animation
      • We spent all semester trying to make great images. Please apply all the developed skills to this project. Create something great!
