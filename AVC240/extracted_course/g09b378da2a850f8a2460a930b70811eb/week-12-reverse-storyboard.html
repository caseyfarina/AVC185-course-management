Please use the 3 frame portrait format, I recommend Photoshop

Storyboard Templates - StudioBinder.zip

Reverse engineer storyboards for every shot in the following, each shot should have a corresponding storyboard frame

  1. Wall-e dancing scene (18 + shots)
  2. West World Conversation scene (21 shots)
  3. A scene of your own choosing. It should be at least a minute and comprise at least 10 shots 
  • Do not spend more than a minute or two sketching each scene. Just capture the gesture and basic visual elements
  • Closely Consider each aspect of Cinematography that we've covered. Please use the previous Studio Binder references when selecting the shot size and shot framing. 
    • MIRO BOARD: https://miro.com/app/board/o9J_l1d3j1s=/?share_link_id=976981777095

Please include the following information for every shot in the storyboard. 

  • shot number - sequentially
  • indicating start time code Minutes: Seconds: Frames (if necessary)
  • Lensing - wide angle, medium 50 - 80, long lens telephoto
  • Shot framing - 1, 2, 3, OTS, Establishing, Insert, POV
  • Shot Size - ECU,CU, MCU, MSI, CS, MFS, FS
  • Camera Movement - Dolly(Push/pull) In/Out, Tilt, Pan, Roll, Zolly, whip pan, hand held
  • Camera Technique - Shallow DOF, Motion Blur - long shutter
  • Lighting Technique - How your create lighting in C4D, animation
