Project Objectives: Students will practice directing view interest using depth of field as a film making technique.

Course Objectives:

  • Stage a scene for a character and/or product shot.
  • Apply the appropriate lines of action to virtual cameras in various environments.
  • Compare and contrast the visualization process in traditional arts vs. directing a shot in a
    digital environment.
  • Manipulate aperture, depth of field, lenses, and attributes needed to control a virtual
    camera in various environments.

Project:

Edit a showcase video in AE with the following elements. 

  • Title card with subtle scale movement
  • DOF 3D pulling focus: Use the lamp scene from last week as the basis for a shot that pulls focus from the foreground object to the midground object.
  • DOF 3D Character pulling focus: Create a scene with Amy and Michelle from Mixamo that pulls focus from Amy in the foreground to Michelle in the background. 
  • DOF 3D: Create a shot of your own design with at least two characters from Mixamo that features a focus pull.
  • Long Shutter Fast Moving Object: Use the flash scene from last week as the basis for a shot that features the running character blurred by a slow shutter speed. You may use render passes and compositing to enhance the shot
  • Long Shutter Fast Camera: Make the camera a child of the running character root object to create a sense of speed by blurring the background. Construct some background elements in the scene to enhance the effect.
