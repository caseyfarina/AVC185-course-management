Project Objective: Students will practice the elements of composition by constructing scenes that leverage camera technique and set design to create compelling imagery,

  • Course Objectives
    • Compare and contrast the visualization process in traditional arts vs. directing a shot in a
      digital environment.
    • Manipulate aperture, depth of field, lenses, and attributes needed to control a virtual
      camera in various environments
    • Apply lighting and compositional concepts to create a mood in a digital environment.
    • Apply the appropriate lines of action to virtual cameras in various environments.
    • Render a project using appropriate project specifications and output methods.
    • Stage a scene for a character and/or product shot.
