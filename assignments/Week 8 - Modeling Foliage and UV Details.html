Objective: Students will model in Blender, UV Unwrap in Blender, Paint in Substance Painter, and export back to Blender.

Course Competencies;

  • Apply basic geometry principles for construction of 3-D models
  • Create 3-D shapes using linear interpolation and polygonal construction
  • Demonstrate ability to modify shape appearance through the use of lights and camera angles. (III)
  • Execute finished models by employing materials, environmental effects, and rendering. (III)
  • Apply basic animation principles to models. (IV)

Project:

  1. Model and Paint some foliage for the pots in the playground.
    1. Use simple topology and overlapping UVs for the leaves.
    2. Use multiple layers and anchor points in SP to texture the foliage
    3. Arrange the foliage asset in several different configurations in each pot so they do look identical
  2. Render a Showcase video of the playground
    1. Playground object requirements
      1. Climbing Animal, Sculpted Animal, Playground structure, slide, spinner, thunder dome, rock, leaf, four pots with plants, 
    2. Video Requirements
      1. Wide Angle Establishing Shot
      2. 3 - 4 orbiting shots of the best models
      3. 3 - 4 topology reveals
      4. Wide Angle Ending Shot
    3. The HDR sky should not be visible in any of the shots, composite a simple gradient as a background in After Effects.
