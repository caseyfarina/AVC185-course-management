Welcome to ART 285

This section combines online, live online and in person students. The in person component of this class takes place on M W 9 - 11:30 AM. You can participate in the online, live online, or in person modalities. You may switch between modalities if you like. The classes are live streamed AND archived on Youtube. If you are not in class in person you are expected to watch the live stream or archived live stream. I will explain this in further detail on Wednesday during the first class period.

In order to be successful in this class you need a computer with a Nvidia GPU. Please follow the directions below to determine if you should borrow a computer (for free) from GCC for the semester (most of you should).

WATCH THIS How do I determine what GPU is in my computer?

  1. Your computer has an Nvidia GPU and is less than four years old. Good! You can use this computer for this class. No need to check out a computer. 
  2. Your computer has an Intel Integrated GPU OR you have a Mac/Apple Computer. You need a properly configured computer for this class. Not addressing this at the beginning of the semester will severely impact your ability to complete this class. 
    1. OPTION 1: check out a laptop from GCC for the semester FOR FREE! Please fill out the checkout form ASAP! You will have to return it at the end of the semester.
      • https://www.gccaz.edu/StudentDeviceRequest
    2. OPTION2: Use the computers in the Digital Media Arts Lab HT1 134.

Participation Requirements
This is an hybrid class, attendance is graded as participation on Canvas.

  • Students must submit a video question/feedback once a week or attend class in person. These
    participation videos can not be submitted late. The Canvas assignment is locked at the deadline.
  • The instructor will answer the student video questions and post a compilation of these answers on the Youtube channel.
