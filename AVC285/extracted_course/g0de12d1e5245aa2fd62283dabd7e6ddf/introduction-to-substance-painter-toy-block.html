What is Substance Painter? A separate piece of software that is designed to make detailed textures and materials MUCH easier. This is the software to use when painting high fidelity materials onto your models. 

What isn't Substance Painter? It is not a modeling tool. All your modeling and UV unwrapping will still need to be done in Cinema 4D.
