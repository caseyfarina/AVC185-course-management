Please upload a professional design brief for your spaceship in PDF format. This should be professionally written (please spell check and use complete sentences). Be sure to include the following components. This ship must be your own original design.

  • Name of the ship
  • Purpose of the ship
  • How does the design influenced by the purpose of the ship?
  • A side view sketch 
  • A top and/or side view sketch
  • These two sketch should be to scale (beginning and end of the craft lines up)
  • Three reference images for the design
  • Three reference images for the surface textures
