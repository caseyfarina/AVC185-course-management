Good start everyone. The forest scenes looked good; a good start. Much of the foliage geometry was too heavy. Leaves, petals, grasses can be a single polygon thick. They do not need two sides. I'll respond on video tomorrow, but you can start on the following videos for improving the leaves, petals, and grasses. Look at the grass and the trees in the asset browser and pay particular attention to the amount of geometry.

  • Some rock ideas: Chris Schmidt and Rocket Lasso are a must follow for C4D. He gets into rocks at the timestamp, however, you should watch the entire talk for some great fracture ideas. https://youtu.be/ENCKIrv9CLI?t=1885
