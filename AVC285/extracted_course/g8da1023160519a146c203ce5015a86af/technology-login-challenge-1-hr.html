CINEMA 4D - The primary software we use for 3D animation

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h9PPiQxurb8

https://www.maxon.net/en/educational-licenses

Please sign up for a educational license for Cinema 4D. If you already have a license check and see when it expires by looking at the  HELP -> LICENSE MANAGER in the C4D application. If you previously downloaded C4D be sure to download the new version. This is the 3D software we will be using in this class. Cinema 4D is produced by a company named "Maxon." The license you will be signing up for is called "Maxon One."  Please use attention to detail throughout this process. There are many steps. It is different than previous semesters for those of you who have previously take my classes. Do not worry if your license runs out during the semester, you can renew your license repeatedly as long as you have an EDU email. 

  1. Create a MyMaxon account. For students that have previously used C4D you will already have a MyMaxon account.
    • https://id.maxon.net/
    • This is a step by step guide through the process 
  2. Create an account at the Kivuto "OnTheHub" eStore.
    • https://www.maxon.net/en/educational-licenses
    • IT WILL COST ABOUT $20!
  3. Download the Maxon App to manage your Maxon downloads
    •  https://www.maxon.net/en/downloads
    • Launch the Maxon App and install the following
      • Cinema 4D (3D modeling and design)
      • Magic Bullet Suite (After Effects Plugin)
      • Trapcode Suite (After Effects Plugin)
      • Universe (After Effects Plugin)
      • VFX Suite (After Effects Plugin)
    • Assign your new Student "Maxon One" license.
  4. Take a screen shot of the HELP -> LICENSE MANAGER in C4D
  5. If you have C4D installed and authorized from a previous class, simply provide a sccreen shot of your license manager in C4D.
