Assignment Overview:

This project invites you to explore high poly sculpting in Blender. Students will use base sculpting tools and alphas to create a rock. Please import the low poly rock into Substance Painter and bake the high poly sculpting details into the mesh maps.

Instruction:

Before you begin, familiarize yourself with the following advanced techniques in Substance Painter:

  • Sculpting in  Blender
    • https://caseyfarina.github.io/lecture-redirects/?class=avc285&lecture=week12-lecture1
    • Create the low poly mesh base and be sure to save. Then sculpt the high poly details. 
  • Substance Painter Review
    • https://caseyfarina.github.io/lecture-redirects/?class=avc285&lecture=week12-lecture2
    • Pay particular attention to the height blend mode. Try not to cover up your stamped details with the height layers of the masked smart materials.
    • Use blur and other effects to minimize the aliasing and dial in the height appearance. 

Course Objectives:

By completing this project, you will achieve the ability to:

  • Apply material to surfaces and 3D models using various painting and mapping
    techniques. (I)
  • Determine illumination effects on a 3D model. (II)
  • Apply various deformation principles for animation and special effects. (II)
  • Produce a 3D character study.

Deliverables:

  1. Canvas Submission: Submit the following in a zip file:

    • A < 4 minute video tour of your Mat texturing and your rock. Explain which techniques you used and explain the low poly to high poly sculpting process.
