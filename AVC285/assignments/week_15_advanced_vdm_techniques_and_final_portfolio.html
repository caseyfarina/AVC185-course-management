Project Overview

This project focusses on the creation and use of sculpting VDM brushes. 

Instruction:

  • Head export worklflow
    • https://youtu.be/YVcb3nYaJqI

Project Objective:

Revise and reimagine your Mat painting by adding the following and improving on last week's design. 

  • Design a Monster head using VDM brushes and other sculpting techniques
  • Export a low poly version of the mesh
  • Bake High Poly details using Substance Painter

Course Objectives:

By completing this project, you will achieve the ability to:

  • Apply material to surfaces and 3D models using various painting and mapping
    techniques. (I)
  • Determine illumination effects on a 3D model. (II)
  • Apply various deformation principles for animation and special effects. (II)
  • Produce a 3D character study.

Deliverables:

  1. Canvas Submission: Submit the following in a zip file:

    • Your final Substance Painter project file for the monster head.
    • A set of rendered images showcasing your textured model from multiple angles. (screen grabs of Substance Painter, or renders from Iray)
    • A < 5 minute video tour of your Monster head sculpt and Paint. Explain which techniques you used, and how the implemented techniques support your artistic vision.
      • Discuss why it is important to use the High Poly -> Bake Details -> Low Poly workflow. 
      • Discuss how sculpted the head, why are VDM brushes useful?
      • Discuss y how you painted your monster head

  2. Discord Engagement: Share your project renders on the designated Discord channel and engage with at least three peers' submissions. Provide constructive feedback focusing on their use of advanced techniques in Substance Painter, and share any insights or discoveries you made during your project.
